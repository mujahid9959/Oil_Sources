Profit Calculation

Using a range approach (minimum to maximum):

Total Revenue (including by-products):
  • Minimum: ₹4,00,000 + ₹20,000 = ₹4,20,000
  • Maximum: ₹10,00,000 + ₹40,000 = ₹10,40,000

  • Minimum Profit: ₹4,20,000 - ₹5,80,000 = -₹1,60,000 (Loss)
